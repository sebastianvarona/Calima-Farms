Our Facility

Our modern packing house uses advanced technology systems to quickly wash, dry, sort and pack large volumes of fruit. Once packed, our products are palletized and stored in high-end cold storage facilities that guarantee freshness and quality throughout the supply chain. The fruit is then loaded into certified containers trucks and dispatched to various domestic and international clients.

Local Operations with a Global Reach

Calima Farm’s packing house is situated in the municipality of La Unión (Valle del Cauca), the epicenter of fruit production in Colombia. From this strategic location, we serve hundreds of local growers while securing a rich variety of high quality fruits year-round. Our unique geographic position also means having access to numerous urban markets and export centers throughout Colombia, including:
