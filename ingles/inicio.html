¿What are we?

Calima Farms is a Colombian agribusiness that specializes in the selection, packing, and global distribution of fresh fruits. We link local growers with international markets to empower and transform rural communities.

Corporate Vision

By 2030, Calima Farms will be recognized as an international leader in the marketing and export of tropical fruits and vegetables. By this date, our agribusiness model will be positioned worldwide as a global benchmark in terms of quality, environmental sustainability, and social value. With holistic corporate strategies and practices, we will empower small and medium-sized producers and contribute to the transformation of Colombia’s countryside.

Commitment to Quality

As a leader in the marketing and packaging of tropical fruits, Calima Farms is committed to strengthening the quality, sustainability and good agricultural practices of each of our products. This dedication not only materializes in a better experience for our clients, it also means a better well-being for our farming communities. Through our rural value chains, we want to continue cultivating an inclusive, innovative, and entrepreneurial development model for the Colombian countryside.

Environmental Sustainability

From the beginning, environmental sustainability has been one of the main priorities of our agribusiness model. This pledge to the environment not only guides our corporate strategies, it also strengthens the quality of our agricultural products and services. Every day, we work to optimize the use of natural resources through actions and processes that reduce our ecological footprint. In this way, we contribute to cultivating a more prosperous and sustainable planet for all.

Labor Standards

At Calima Farms, we recognize that the success of our company lies in the wellbeing of each of our employees. We take meaningful measures to promote a safe and healthy work environment that favors inclusion and mutual respect for all. This commitment also means taking significant steps to ensure that our labor standards meet and exceed the obligations established by national and international laws.
