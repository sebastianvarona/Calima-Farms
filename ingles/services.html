Servicios

Agricultural Services

Calima Farms offers an extensive portfolio of services aimed at the agricultural sector of Colombia and Latin America. Our services include:

Selection & Packing

Our company has the technology and infrastructure to wash, select and pack large quantities of fruit quickly and safely. We meet national and international standards for fruit handling, processing, storage and distribution. In addition, we are able to customized orders based on the varieties, calibers, and volumes that our customers need.

Cold Chain Management

Through a robust logistics system, Calima Farms ships, tracks, and handles large volumes of fresh products throughout Colombia. We manage a comprehensive network of cold rooms and certified transporters that control the quality of our products throughout the supply chain.

Consulting Solutions

With decades of experience and an extensive global network, Calima Farms helps its clients mitigate risks and generate opportunities in Latin America’s growing agricultural sector. We deliver practical solutions towards a more sustainable and inclusive rural development model. Our clients include a wide range of government entities, multilateral institutions, foreign companies, technology startups, and producer associations.
